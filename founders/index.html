Founders

A long, long time ago, before Zayn left One Direction, before the Apple Watch, before Nicki asked Miley “What’s good?,” and before Donald Trump came forward to fix America, ten UTCS students decided to venture where none had gone before. After a largely positive response to the idea of a CS fraternity, these students got to work. The result was UT’s first co-ed Computer Science fraternity, Lambda Alpha Nu. It is to these ten founders we extend our gratitude for establishing this organization.


Reid McKenzie

VP of Administration

I pick locks, play tabletop games, predict branches and compile code. I wanted to help start Texas LAN because after three years in the CS department I wanted an extracurricular social group where I could meet and chill with other people from the major.
