Service

LAN aims to create a positive social environment and build a community of strong friendships, high ambitions, and drive for scholarship. Community service and CS outreach play a major role in the fraternity's mission.


Coder Dojo

Lambda Alpha Nu is proud to be partnered with CoderDojo Round Rock. We provide mentors that help facilitate the weekly meet ups by offering help and programming assistance to attendees of all ages.
